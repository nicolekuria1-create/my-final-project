Drama Club

What we do: Acting classes, stagecraft, script-writing, improv nights, production design, and community performances.

Fun activities: Weekly acting workshops, themed improv evenings, annual play production, backstage tours, costume-making sessions, and storytelling slams.

Cost to join: GHS 30 per term (covers props & basic costume supplies). Optional production fees may apply for larger shows.

Meeting: Wednesdays 4:00pm–6:00pm (Auditorium)

How to Join

  1. Click the "Join" button on the club you want.
  2. Enter your name and select the club (and activity if applicable).
  3. Pay the listed term fee at the administration office or via the school payment portal.
  4. You'll receive a confirmation and meeting details via email.
