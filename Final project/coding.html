About the Coding Club

The Coding Club teaches practical programming skills: web development, Python, algorithms, and project-based learning. We focus on building real projects and preparing for competitions.

What we do

  • Weekly workshops on web, Python, and data basics
  • Group projects: websites, games, and utilities
  • Hackathon practice and competitive programming sessions
  • Guest talks and industry mentorship

Why join?

Coding develops problem-solving, logical thinking, and teamwork. Members build portfolios, collaborate on projects, and gain skills useful for careers and study.

Meetings

Tuesdays 3:30pm–5:00pm in the computer lab. Special sessions on weekends before competitions.
