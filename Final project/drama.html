About the Drama Club

The Drama Club focuses on acting, voice, movement, and stage production. We explore classical and contemporary plays, devise original pieces, and collaborate with the school community to produce memorable performances.

What we do

  • Weekly rehearsals and acting workshops
  • Scriptwriting and improvisation sessions
  • Costume and set design workshops
  • Community performances and festivals

Why join?

Participating in drama builds confidence, public speaking skills, teamwork, and creativity. Whether you want to perform on stage, help behind the scenes, or learn how to write and direct, drama offers space to grow.
