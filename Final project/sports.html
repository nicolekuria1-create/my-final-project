About the Sports Club

The Sports Club organizes training, inter-school matches, and fitness sessions. We welcome players of all abilities and emphasise fair play, teamwork and personal growth.

What we do

  • Training sessions for football, basketball, athletics and more
  • Weekly fitness and conditioning workshops
  • Friendly matches and inter-school tournaments
  • Leadership and teamwork development activities

Why join?

Sports improve health, resilience and communication skills. Members build friendships, represent the school, and gain leadership opportunities.

Meetings

Mondays & Thursdays 4:00pm–6:00pm at the sports field/gym. Tryouts before tournament season.
